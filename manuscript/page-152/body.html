PUCK, November 19th, 1890.

Subsequent events have proved that there was no mistake made in attributing the Republican defeat of 1890 to the effect of the McKinley Bill. Puck of November 19th, in enumerating various possible causes for the turn-over, says to the Republicans: Do not distress yourselves to decide which sort of cake gave you the stomach-ache.
