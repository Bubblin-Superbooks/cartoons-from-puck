On The Road.

Conkling.—Want a guide, sir? Garfield.—No; thank you!

PUCK, February 2nd, 1881.

This cartoon sketches fairly the situation a month before Mr. Garfield’s inauguration in 1881. Mr. Conkling had shown a certain willingness to lend a hand to Mr. Garfield’s administration, and Mr. Garfield had shown no willingness whatever to accept the proffered hand.
